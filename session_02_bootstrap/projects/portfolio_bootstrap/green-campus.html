Green Campus –
“Green Action Tracking” System

Green Campus – “Green Action Tracking” System is a university team project developed for the Software Engineering course, aiming to build a system that records and encourages environmentally friendly activities within the campus community.

In this project, I was responsible for requirement analysis and system documentation. My responsibilities included gathering and analyzing requirements, writing Use Case Specifications, and designing system models such as FDD, DFD, ERD, Use Case Diagrams, and Sequence Diagrams to describe system workflows and functionalities.

Through this project, I strengthened my analytical thinking, documentation, communication, and teamwork skills. The experience also helped me gain practical knowledge of software development processes and reinforced my interest in pursuing a career as a Business Analyst.
